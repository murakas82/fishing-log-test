Privacy Policy

How this fishing log app handles your data.

1. Who operates this app?

This fishing log application is a personal hobby project operated by Ilmar Murakas. It is not a commercial service.

If you have questions about privacy or data, you can reach me at: ilmar.murakas@gmail.com.

2. What data is collected?

The app stores the minimum amount of data needed to provide its functionality. This includes:

  • Account data – your email address used for login (via Firebase Authentication). Passwords are handled and stored only by Firebase and are never visible to the app code.
  • Fishing spots – names and GPS coordinates of spots you save.
  • Catches – information you enter such as species, weight, lure, notes and optional weather information associated with the catch.
  • Weather data – when you log a catch, the app may store the current weather (temperature, wind, pressure, etc.) fetched from a third-party weather API.

3. How is data used?

The data you enter is used only to:

  • Display your fishing spots and catch history.
  • Calculate simple statistics and predictions based on your own data.
  • Show local weather and forecasts for your saved spots using external weather APIs.

Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with advertisers.

4. Where is data stored?

The app uses Google Firebase for authentication and data storage:

  • Firebase Authentication – stores your login credentials securely and manages your sign-in session.
  • Firebase Firestore – stores your spots, catches and related metadata in a cloud database.

All access to your data from the app is tied to your authenticated user account.

5. Cookies and local storage

The app uses essential cookies and local storage for:

  • Keeping you logged in with Firebase Authentication.
  • Remembering that you dismissed the cookie notice.

No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

6. Third-party services

The app relies on external services to provide maps and weather:

  • Firebase (Google) – authentication and database.
  • OpenStreetMap + Leaflet – map tiles and geographic search.
  • Open-Meteo (and/or other free weather APIs) – current weather and forecast for your locations.

These services may receive your IP address and the location coordinates you request in order to return map tiles or weather data.

7. Your rights and control

Because this is a personal hobby app and not a commercial product, no automated account management is implemented yet. However, you can:

  • Delete individual spots and catches using the app UI.
  • If you want your entire account and data removed, contact: ilmar.murakas@gmail.com.

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated as the app evolves or if features change significantly. The latest version will always be available on this page.

This document is provided for transparency and convenience and is not formal legal advice.
